Frequently Asked Questions

How are the lessons going to be conducted

Apprentice Lab offers all its courses 100% online so as to cater for persons over the world, Lessons will be conducted twice a week and we offer specialized schedules for everyone

What programming languages are taught in the web development course?

Our web development course covers a range of programming languages, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frameworks such as React.js and Node.js.

Can beginners with no prior coding experience enroll in the UX/UI design course?

Absolutely! Our UX/UI design course is tailored to accommodate beginners with no prior coding experience. We start from the basics, covering fundamental design principles and tools before progressing to more advanced topics.

Is there any prerequisite knowledge required for the mobile app development course?

While prior programming experience is helpful, it's not mandatory. Our mobile app development course caters to learners of all levels, providing comprehensive instruction from app ideation to deployment.

Are the courses self-paced, or do they follow a structured schedule?

Our courses follow a structured schedule with set start and end dates. However, we understand that learners have different schedules, so we provide recorded lectures and supplementary materials for self-paced learning.

Is there any career support provided after completing the courses?

Yes, we offer career support services to our students, including resume building, interview preparation, and job placement assistance. Our goal is to help students transition smoothly into the tech industry.

What are the payment options available for course fees?

We offer flexible payment options to accommodate students' needs. Payment can be made in full upfront or through installment plans. Additionally, we accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and online payment platforms, to ensure convenience for our students
